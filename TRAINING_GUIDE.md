## Off-Season Phase (October - December)
Philosophy: Mental recovery + maintain intensity + build strength
Volume: 4-8 hours/week, mostly unstructured

ISO Week 41 -> ISO Week 52

Workout Structure:
2-3 unstructured rides per week
1 intensity session (VO2 Max, Threshold, Sweet Spot), number of interval/interval length dictated by time avaible and best coaching practises for elite cyclists.

Workout Length:
1-2 hours depending on how time constriant atheletes are.

Workout difficulty progression:
No structured progression
